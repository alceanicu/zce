- [ ] A)
```php
stream_set_timeout()
```

- [ ] B)
```php
stream_get_meta_data()
```

- [ ] C)
```php
stream_set_read_buffer()
```

- [ ] D)
```php
stream_set_time()
```


<details><summary><b>Answer</b></summary>
<p>
  Answer: <strong>A</strong>

</details>
