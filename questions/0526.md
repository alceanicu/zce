```php
<select name="?????" multiple>
    <option value="1">Item #1</option>
    <!-- ... more options ... -->
</select>
```

- [ ] A) myselectbox[]
- [ ] B) myselectbox=array()
- [ ] C) myselectbox['multiple']
- [ ] D) myselectbox{'multiple'}
