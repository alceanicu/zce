```php

}

a($oranges);
echo "I have $apples apples and $oranges oranges";
```

- [ ] A) I have 5 apples and 6 oranges

- [ ] C) I have 5 apples and 5 oranges
- [ ] D) I have 6 apples and 6 oranges
