What is the correct way to add 1 to the `$count` variable?

- [ ] A)
```php
<?php $count++;
```

- [ ] B)
```php
<?php $count +=  1;
```

- [ ] C)
```php
<?php  ++count
```

- [ ] D)
```php
<?php  count++
```


<details><summary><b>Answer</b></summary>
<p>
  Answer: <strong>A, B</strong>
</p>
</details>
