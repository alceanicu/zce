Consider the following script:

```php
$string1 = "ab";
$string2 = "cd";
$string1 = $string1 . $string2; 
$string3 = "abc";
$string1 .= $string3;
echo $string1;
```
What will be the output of the above PHP script?

- [ ] A) abcdabc
- [ ] B) cdabcab
- [ ] C) abc
- [ ] D) cdabc

<details><summary><b>Answer</b></summary>
<p>
  Answer: <strong>A</strong>
</p>
</details>
