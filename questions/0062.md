Consider the following code:

```php
$x = 10;
$y = 5;
$x += $y;
```
What will be the value of $x and $y?

- [ ] A) x=15, y=5
- [ ] B) x=10, y=5
- [ ] C) x=15, y=10
- [ ] D) x=5, y=15

<details><summary><b>Answer</b></summary>
<p>
  Answer: <strong>A</strong>
</p>
</details>
