How do you obtain the length of a string in PHP ?

- [ ] A)
```php
echo strlen($string);
```

- [ ] B)
```php
echo $string->length;
```

- [ ] C)
```php
echo count($string);
```

- [ ] D)
```php
echo sizeof($string);
```


<details><summary><b>Answer</b></summary>
<p>
  Answer: <strong>A</strong>
</p>
</details>
