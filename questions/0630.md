When comparing two strings, which of the following is acceptable?

- [ ] A)
```php
$a === $b;
```

- [ ] B)
```php
strcasecmp($a, $b);
```

- [ ] C)
```php
strcmp($a, $b);
```

- [ ] D)
```php
$a == $b;
```


<details><summary><b>Answer</b></summary>
<p>
  Answer: <strong>A, B, C, D</strong>
</p>
</details>
