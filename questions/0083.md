Which of the following is the correct naming convention of user defined functions?

- [ ] A)
```php
&sum($var1, $var2)
```

- [ ] B)
```php
_sum($var1, $var2)
```

- [ ] C)
```php
123_sum($var1,$var2)
```

- [ ] D)
```php
^^_sum($var1, $var2)
```


<details><summary><b>Answer</b></summary>
<p>
  Answer: <strong>A, B</strong>
</p>
</details>
